About HoneyInsured

Founded by the friendliest data nerds, HoneyInsured strives to put the best computer technology on your side for a change. Our user-friendly apps can instantly estimate subsidies and health costs, track physicians, and even find the best plans for expectant mothers or self-employed workers. HoneyInsured uses all of the technology and data that corporations use to put the most powerful health technology where it belongs - at your fingertips.
