Beat the System, Get Cheap Health Insurance in 5 min.

October 27, 2015 | Grace Gee

Depending on your income and household size, you may be eligible for tax subsidies (aka tax credits) or cost-sharing benefits for On-Exchange Plans. We automatically help you apply for federal tax subsidies to make your health insurance more affordable. No need to spend hours on HealthCare.gov!

  • Tax subsidies: Tax subsidies lowers your monthly cost. They are paid by the government your health insurance company on a monthly basis. You may qualify for tax subsidies if your 2016 income is below 4 times the federal poverty level:
    • You’re an individual making below $47.1K.
    • You’re a family of 2 making below $63.7K.
    • You’re a family of 3 making below $80.4K.
    • You’re a family of 4 making below $97K.
  • Cost-sharing Reduction: In addition to the tax subsidies help, if your 2016 income is below 2.5 times the federal poverty level, you also get lower deductibles and copay benefits on silver plans.
    • You’re an individual making below $29.4K.
    • You’re a family of 2 making below $39.8K..
    • You’re a family of 3 making below $50.2K.
    • You’re a family of 4 making below $60.6K.

More than likely, you’re eligible for help. 87% of people who signed up for On-Exchange plan in 2015 got tax subsidies. On average, these people got $263 per person per month in government assistance, that’s $12,624 of annual help for a family of four.

The amount of tax subsidies and cost-sharing varies by state. To find out if you qualify for either tax subsidies or cost-sharing reduction, type in your zip code below to get started. On your plans page, we automatically calculate your plan cost and benefits based on your family size and income. HoneyInsured can also enroll you in 5 minutes including tax subsidies and cost-sharing.
