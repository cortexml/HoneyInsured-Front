5 Important Dates to Know for Open Enrollment

October 27, 2015 | Grace Gee

Don’t miss any deadlines to get affordable health insurance. Buying insurance is not like buying Walmart toilet paper 24/7, insurance is not available all-year-round. Here are the dates you need to know to be covered for 2016.Don’t miss any deadlines to get affordable health insurance. We offer health insurance in TX, FL, VA, GA, MO, NC, OH, PA.

  • November 1, 2015: This is the first day of Open Enrollment. On November 1, 2015, you can enroll in an insurance plan that starts coverage on January 1, 2016.
  • December 15, 2015: This is the last day to enroll for coverage that starts on January 1, 2016.
  • January 15, 2016: This is the last day to enroll for coverage that starts on February 1, 2016.
  • January 31, 2016: This is the last day of Open Enrollment. This is also the last day to enroll for coverage that starts on March 1, 2016.
  • After January 31, 2016:
    1. If you haven’t enrolled in a plan by January 31, 2016, you cannot enroll in health insurance unless you have a special qualifying event.* These events include marriage/divorce, adoption, moving to a new zip code, etc.
    2. If you don’t qualify for a hardship exemption,** you may be legally required to pay tax penalties of the greater of the following
      1. $695/adult, $347.50/child (family max $2,085)
      2. or 2.5% of your household income that is above the tax return filing threshold for your filing status
